# Rules for Tracking & Analyzing Big Money / Institutional Flow in Indonesia Stock Market (IDX)

**Last updated:** March 2026  
**Focus:** Foreign institutional flow (main driver of IHSG & blue chips) + visible domestic institutional behavior

## 1. Core Data Sources – Daily / Free

| Priority | Source                  | What You Get                              | Best For                          | Access Path / App                          |
|----------|-------------------------|-------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------|--------------------------------------------|
| 1        | idx.co.id               | Official Net Foreign Buy/Sell (market-wide) | Overall market sentiment         | Data Pasar → Laporan Statistik → Equity Trading by Investor |

## 2. Key Interpretation Rules

### Market-wide Rules
- Net foreign buy > **Rp 1–2 T** in a week               → strong bullish institutional signal
- Consistent net buy 5–10 trading days + IHSG rising    → accumulation / uptrend phase
- Sudden large net sell (> Rp 1.5–2 T in 1–2 days)      → risk-off / potential correction

### Per-Stock Rules (most important for trading)
1. **Rising foreign flow line** (Stockbit) while price consolidates/sideways → **quiet accumulation** → highest probability breakout setup
2. **Foreign Flow + Price alignment**
   - Flow ↑ & Price ↑     → strong confirmed uptrend
   - Flow ↑ & Price flat  → institutions accumulating → watch for breakout
   - Flow ↓ & Price ↑     → distribution / weakening → potential reversal
   - Flow ↓ & Price ↓     → confirmed downtrend / avoid longs
3. **Foreign Flow Strength** (green = flow actually driving price)
   - Prefer stocks with **green strength bars** during inflow
4. **Divergence watch**
   - Flow turns positive while price still falling → possible bottoming
   - Flow turns negative while price still rising → possible topping

## 3. Practical Daily Routine (Jakarta traders standard – 5–10 min)

1. **07:30–08:30 WIB**  
   Check idx.co.id → Net Foreign (yesterday & week-to-date)
2. **Open Stockbit or RTI**  
   Look at “Top Net Foreign Buy” list (10–20 names)
3. **Filter & Chart**  
   - Pick 3–5 names with biggest buy volume  
   - Add Foreign Flow Underlay + Net Buy histogram  
   - Check if flow is **rising 2–5 days** already
4. **Cross-check**  
   - Volume increasing?  
   - Fundamental story / news catalyst?  
   - Domestic also buying (RTI “Domestic” column surging)?
5. **Trade only when flow direction agrees** with your technical setup

## 4. Advanced / Confirmation Signals

- **Block trades** / large single transactions (RTI or broker)
- **Foreign ownership % rising** quarter-on-quarter (Fundachart)
- **Sector rotation** — which sectors get consistent foreign inflow
- **Global context** — watch USD/IDR, US yields, China markets (foreign flow reverses fast on risk-off)

## 5. Risk & Psychology Rules

- **Never fight the flow** — most big losses happen when fighting institutional selling
- **Foreign can exit in 1–3 days** — always use trailing stop / hard stop
- **Domestic institutions** can support temporarily → but rarely overpower sustained foreign selling
- **Best setups** = foreign inflow + domestic inflow + technical breakout
- **Avoid** = heavy foreign selling even if price looks “cheap”
